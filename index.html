Activities

Indoor Restore & Renew

Indoor Restore & Renew yoga is held daily in our sanctuary cabin complete with meditation bells. From beginner to advanced, enjoy an enlightening experience on the mat. Mats and blocks are available for you.

Find Your Ground

If you enjoy being one with nature our outdoor Find Your Ground yoga are held daily. Outside on our private deck, mats and blocks are available for you. From beginner to advanced, learn how to ground yourself on the mat, and in life.

Calming Views

Book our “Calming Views” rooms with many windows to see the gorgeous views right from your bed. Each room has a single king bed with ultimate comfort. All of our “Views For You” rooms are pet friendly.

Book our “Cozy Cabin” rooms for a more secluded feel. These rooms are two stories. Both stories have a twin bed and a desk, with a special cozy vibe.
